Sanger Method Theory
Chain Termination:
ddNTPs lack 3'-OH group, preventing further extension when incorporated by DNA polymerase
Four Reactions:
Separate reactions with each ddNTP create fragments ending at specific bases
Gel Electrophoresis:
Fragments separated by size; sequence read from bottom (shortest) to top (longest)
